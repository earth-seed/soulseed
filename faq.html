FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

When and where is the festival?

SoulSeed Festival takes place from July 11-13, 2025, in Yenice Vadi, Turkey. The exact location details will be shared with ticket holders.

What's included in the ticket?

Your ticket includes access to all festival areas, workshops, ceremonies, and performances. Camping is included, but food and beverages are separate.

What should I bring?

Essential items include camping gear, comfortable clothing for various weather conditions, reusable water bottle, and any personal items you may need. A detailed packing list will be provided to ticket holders.

Is there accommodation available?

We offer camping areas included with your ticket. For those seeking additional comfort, we have glamping options and partnerships with nearby accommodations available for an extra fee.

What's the food situation?

The festival features various food vendors offering local, organic, and international cuisine. Vegetarian and vegan options are available. You're also welcome to bring your own food.

How do I get there?

We provide shuttle services from nearby airports and cities. Detailed transportation information and directions will be shared with ticket holders.

What's the refund policy?

Tickets are refundable up to 60 days before the event. Within 60 days, tickets can be transferred to another person but are non-refundable.
