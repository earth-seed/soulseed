Location

Nestled in the heart of Turkey's pristine nature, Yenice Vadi is a sacred valley where ancient wisdom meets natural beauty. This enchanting location provides the perfect backdrop for our transformative festival experience.

Surrounded by majestic mountains and blessed with crystal-clear streams, this valley has been a gathering place for spiritual seekers and nature lovers for generations. Its serene atmosphere and powerful energy create an ideal environment for deep connection and inner exploration.

The valley's natural amphitheaters and peaceful groves will host our ceremonies, workshops, and performances, while its starlit nights will witness our community gatherings and celebrations.
